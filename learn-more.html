Validation Action Plan

A roadmap, not just a rating.

ValidateHQ ends every report with ordered, actionable steps to reduce uncertainty before you burn months of runway on the wrong assumptions.

01

Conduct market research

Run 15–20 discovery interviews with target customers to validate whether the pain point is real, urgent, and worth paying for.

02

Validate core assumptions

Build a landing page or concierge test to measure real demand signals before writing a line of product code.

03

Build a focused MVP

Scope the smallest version that delivers the core value. Get it in front of 10 users within 6 weeks and iterate ruthlessly.

04

Measure and iterate

Define one north-star metric. Track it weekly. Let data — not gut feel — drive the next move.
